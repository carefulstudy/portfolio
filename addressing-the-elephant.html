Addressing the Elephant!

Let’s face it—the current state of online gaming makes me sad. Too many developers have turned their focus toward exploiting players with microtransactions, prioritizing profit over creating genuine, enjoyable experiences. It’s disappointing to see games prey on their own communities, chipping away at what made gaming so magical in the first place.

Creating Psi Freak has been my way of fighting back. After years of development, countless hours of reflection, and, yes, a few manic episodes along the way, I knew this had to be different. I’m pouring everything into this game because it’s all I have, and because giving back to players like you means everything to me. It’s my way to keep myself grounded while trying to make a meaningful difference in gaming.

This isn’t just a game; it’s a world where players can escape, explore, and experience something real—without ever being asked for another cent. Inspired by my memories of games like MapleStory, RuneScape, and World of Warcraft, and my unbreakable love for my old Game Boy, I’m blending the best of both worlds to bring you a universe that I hope will rekindle your love for gaming.

Right now, Psi Freak is available for Windows, but with enough support, I’d love to bring it to the Nintendo Switch, too. Thank you for being here and helping me create something meaningful. Together, we can make gaming a better place for everyone.
