Give & Glow

Hey there, awesome friend! Do you want to lend a hand? That would be fantastic! I truly admire those who take the time to help others, especially when it comes to our shared gaming experience. If you own a serial code for Psi Freak and are willing to share your gamepad profile with me, you can earn something special: the Divine Spark!

By sending me your controller mapping, you’ll not only contribute to our community by helping others find the perfect setup for their gaming adventures, but you’ll also receive the Divine Spark in-game!

This spark symbolizes your generosity and your initiative to make gaming smoother for everyone. It signifies that you’ve reached out to help others, and your selflessness deserves to be recognized!

So, if you’d like to share your gamepad profile, just send it my way, and I’ll add it to the downloads page for everyone to benefit. Your kindness will be rewarded, and together we’ll make Psi Freak an even better place for all players. Thank you for being part of this incredible journey!

The spark is located in the upper-right corner, and can be seen by anyone.
