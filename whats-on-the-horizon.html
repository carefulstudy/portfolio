Focused Testing in Budding Town.
Right now, I'm keeping the game world small by limiting it to the starter town. This approach helps me focus on fine-tuning server stability and player scaling, so I can tackle one challenge at a time and ensure the smoothest experience possible. My main focus is on the game’s foundation, so there won’t be regular social media updates for a bit. But keep your eyes peeled!

What's next?
Get ready to explore the untamed paths beyond Budding Town, where fields stretch far and winding roads lead to new challenges! Stay sharp—wild creatures and other Psi Freaks might want to test your skills! The leaderboards are set, but first, the roadblock north of Budding Town needs to clear before your climb begins!
