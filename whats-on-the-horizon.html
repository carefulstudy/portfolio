What’s on the Horizon!

Notice: This page will be frequently updated, so don’t forget to follow our official Twitter and Instagram for the latest news!

Focused Testing in the Starter Town.
Right now, we’re keeping the game world small by limiting it to the starter town. This approach helps us focus on fine-tuning server stability and player scaling, so we can tackle one challenge at a time and ensure the smoothest experience possible. Our main focus is on perfecting the game’s foundation, so there won’t be regular social media updates for a bit. But keep your eyes peeled!

What’s Next?
Starting January 1st, we’re excited to roll out a high score leaderboard on our website, showcasing the top players’ achievements! This feature will highlight the best of the best, giving everyone a chance to aim for the top spots!

In this next phase of testing, the game world will grow beyond the starter town to include turn-based matches against wild creatures, as well as intense battles with other fighters. Along with battles, players can expect to dive into more areas to explore as the world opens up in carefully planned chunks. Get ready to test your skills, traverse the wild grass, and rise to the challenge!

Known issues:

Server Stability: Crashes or downtime affecting accessibility.

Latency Issues: High ping causing gameplay delays.

Connection Errors: Server dropping players.

Gamepad Support: Check out the gamepad manual for solutions.
