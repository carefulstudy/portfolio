Psi Freak FAQs

Can someone tell me what Psi Freak is?

Well, a Psi Freak is a super cool name for someone who has special powers called psionics! There are more than 8 different types of psionics, and each type can control something awesome, like heat, sound, or moisture. These amazing people are the stars of the game!

Why are some places in the world closed off or fenced in?

The game has only been worked on in a small, local setting by the developer for three years. This means Psifreak has not been tested in an online setting. To make sure everything works well, I’ll slowly add new parts to the game one at a time. This helps me fix any problems and make each part really great before adding more.

Is it for kids, grown-ups, or both?

Psifreak is rated "E for Everyone," which means it’s great for kids aged 6 and up. The game involves playful fights using items like yo-yos and water balloons, and even use cool powers like telekinesis, ensuring a kid-friendly and entertaining experience.

Can you spend real money in Psi Freak?

No, Psi Freak is all about honing your powers, not throwing money at them. I want to ensure the experience is fair and fun for everyone. No shortcuts through spending. I'm focused on creating a complete game without additional purchases to bog down the experience.


Will Psi Freak be available on systems apart from Windows?

Right now, Psi Freak will only be available on Windows computers. I want to make it available on other consoles like Nintendo Switch, but the core technology is being significantly updated, and maintaining multiple platforms would take too much time. I'll revisit it in the future!

Will Psi Freak be available on Steam?

Right now, Psi Freak is only available on itch.io. I'm choosing to stick with itch.io because it gives me complete control over the game. This way, I can create the game just the way we want it. So, for now, it’s staying on itch.io.


Where can I get more of Psi Freak?

If you're looking to dive deeper into the world of Psi Freak, you’ll definitely want to check out Psi Freak's Instagram page! There you will find the latest news, behind-the-scenes peeks, and cool updates. Just hit that follow button to stay in the loop and never miss out on the action! You might even catch some exclusive content or sneak previews—perfect for any super-fan like you!
