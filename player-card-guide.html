Understanding Your Player Card: PSK and PUK Codes
Let’s take a closer look at your Player Card. At the bottom, inside the red rectangle, you’ll find a 12-character PSK. This code serves as your unique identifier, granting you access to your account and all its features. At the top-left corner, you’ll see an 8-character PUK Code. This code is your safety net, allowing you to recover your account or reset your PIN if necessary. It is crucial to keep these codes secure.

1. Start up Psi Freak by double-clicking the executable file inside your Psi Freak folder. This will open up the authorization screen.

2. Locate the 12-character PIK code inside the white rectangle at the bottom of your card and enter these 12 characters using the keypad.

3. Locate the 4-character PUK code at the top-left corner of your Player Card and enter these characters using the keypad.

4. Now that you’ve unlocked the "Set PIN" feature, type in your new PIN. Make sure it’s secure, yet easy for you to remember.

5. Once your new PIN has been accepted, your Player Card is successfully set. You can now log in using your new PIN code.

6. For added security, make sure to keep your Player Card and PIN information in a safe place to prevent unauthorized access.
