Demo Guide

Welcome to the world of Psi Freak! To get started, follow these simple instructions:

Downloading and Installing the Game

To launch Psi Freak, visit our Itch.io page and click the "Download" button to grab the .tar file. Once it’s downloaded, find it in your downloads folder and drag it to your desktop. Right-click the .tar file, select "Extract All" to unzip it, and open the extracted folder. Double-click the executable file inside, and you’re ready to jump into the action!

Login and Starting the Demo
When you launch the game, you'll be greeted by the login screen with a security numpad. Use the arrow keys to navigate and enter "DEMO" in the serial-key field. Once entered, you’re ready to jump into the action!

Customizing Your Character
Next, you’ll be taken to the character creation screen. Here, you can customize your character to your liking. You will have the option to adjust:

Sex (Choose between male or female)

Age (Adjust your character's age)

Color (Select your character’s skin tone)

Meeting Professor Lennart
After creating your character, you will be greeted by the renowned Professor Lennart. He will ask for your name—choose wisely, as this will be your identity in the game! Once you have provided your name, Professor Lennart will welcome you to the new world of Psi Freak. Your journey is about to begin!

The Starter Town – Where Adventures Begin
Welcome to Budding Town, where new adventures are born! Nestled in the quiet countryside, this small village feels like something out of a postcard—a place where everyone knows your name, and strange things sometimes lurk behind the everyday. The locals are friendly, but there’s an odd energy in the air, hinting that bigger things lie just beyond the horizon.

For now, the roads out of town are blocked, but soon you'll venture into the wilds, using your unique psionic powers—controlling sound, water, and heat with just a thought. Armed with everyday items, you'll face off against other psi freaks and uncover secrets hiding just beneath the surface of this peaceful place. Get ready, because the calm of Budding Town won't last long!
